## KYC reliance

Blink supports KYC reliance, so verified users can move through fiat checkout with minimal repeated verification.

### Why this matters

Without KYC reliance, users often face:

- Repeated document upload flows
- Slower onboarding to first deposit
- Higher friction and drop-off
- Lower conversion from intent to funded wallet

### How Blink KYC reliance works

1. Your app opens the Blink deposit session.
2. Blink handles the required identity verification flow directly in checkout.
3. Blink completes KYC checks when required by amount, route, and compliance rules.
4. Blink finalizes the fiat deposit and funds the destination wallet.
5. Your app receives the funded-user outcome without running a separate KYC flow.

<Info>
KYC is completed once per user with Blink. If a user already passed Blink KYC in another app, they typically do not need to complete full KYC again in your app.
</Info>

### Integration notes

- No separate KYC provider integration is required in your app.
- Keep your deposit trigger and destination parameters server-validated.
- Handle completion and error events so users can retry failed deposits cleanly.
- Use Blink status and webhook data for reconciliation in your backend.
