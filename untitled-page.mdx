### What is Blink.cash?

Blink is the easiest way to deposit into any onchain app. Set it up once, and every deposit after that is one tap.

This means no chains, no tokens, and no sending funds manually from your wallet. Blink handles all of it without ever taking custody of your funds. 

Think of it as your spending account for onchain apps.

### Why use Blink?

1. **It's the easiest way to try apps and keep funding them.** No chains, no tokens, no deposit addresses, no manual sends. Just one tap and you're in!
2. **It's secure.** Blink creates a scoped separation between where you hold your money and the apps you're trying out. Your wallet stays sealed. Only what you've approved can move, and only when you say so.
